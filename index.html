Thoughts

Short notes on tech, design, and figuring things out.

Mar 2026
Design
Good UI is invisible. You only notice it when it's missing — like a door with no handle. Most engineers think "it works" is enough. It's not. How it feels to use is also part of how it works.
Feb 2026
Engineering
Spent 3 hours debugging a soil moisture sensor that was reading wrong. Turned out it was upside down. The lesson wasn't "check orientation first" — it was to question your assumptions before touching the code.
Jan 2026
AI
LLMs are strange tools — the better your question, the better the answer. That's not a new idea. Good engineers have always known that framing the problem correctly is half the solution.
Dec 2025
Football
Watched a game where the better team lost because they didn't adapt at halftime. Product teams do this constantly — fall in love with the original plan and ignore what the scoreboard is saying. Read the room. Adjust.
